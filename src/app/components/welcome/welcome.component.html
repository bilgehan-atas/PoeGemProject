Welcome to poe.thaumaturgy!

A tool for automatically calculating the profitability of crafting gems in Path of Exile. This tool allows Path of Exile players to determine whether it's profitable to craft gems, by automatically fetching the current market prices for the required items from poe.ninja. The tool calculates the cost of crafting the gem compared to its expected market value, and shows whether crafting the gem is likely to result in a profit or a loss. This help players make informed decisions about their crafting investments without the need for manual input of data.

Key Features
  • Automatically fetches the current market prices for the base gem, the quality gem, and any additional crafting materials from poe.ninja.
  • Calculates the cost of crafting a gem based on the fetched prices.
  • Estimates the expected market value of the crafted gem.
  • Determines whether crafting the gem is likely to result in a profit or a loss.
  • Helps players make informed decisions about gem crafting investments.
How to Use
  • Select the league from dropdown menu.
  • The tool will automatically fetch the current market prices for the base gem, the quality gem, and any additional crafting materials from poe.ninja.
  • The tool will calculate the estimated cost of crafting the gem and its expected market value.
  • The result will indicate whether crafting the gem is likely to result in a profit or a loss.
Additional Information
  • This tool is intended for use with the game Path of Exile and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the game's developers or poe.ninja.
  • The accuracy of the tool's calculations depends on the accuracy of the data fetched from poe.ninja and the current market conditions, and should be used as a guide only.
  • This tool does not provide investment advice and users should exercise their own judgment and discretion when making investment decisions.
